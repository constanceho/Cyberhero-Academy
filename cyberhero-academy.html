🔑 Password Power

What's a password?

A password is a secret word that protects your stuff online — like the lock on your treasure chest! 🗝️ If a villain guesses it, they can read your messages, change your game, or worse.

A strong password is long, mixes BIG and small letters, has numbers, and adds symbols like ! or #. Don't use your name or "12345"!

🔒 Secret Keeper

What is "personal information"?

Personal information is anything that tells someone who you are or where to find you. CyberHeroes keep this stuff safe! 🛡️

If something could help a stranger find you in real life, it's private. If it's just fun stuff anyone could know, it's usually okay to share.

👀 Stranger Sense

People online aren't always who they say they are

Some people online pretend to be kids when they're really not — that's called "catfishing." 🐟 Real friends don't ask for secrets, photos, or to meet without your parents knowing.

The Golden Rule: If someone online makes you feel weird, scared, or asks you to keep secrets from grown-ups — STOP and tell a trusted adult right away!

🎣 Phish Buster

What is "phishing"?

Phishing (sounds like "fishing" 🎣) is when bad guys send fake messages trying to trick you into giving away passwords or clicking dangerous links. They use bait — like saying you've won a prize!

Phishing clues:
🚨 Saying it's URGENT or you'll lose something
🎁 Promising free stuff or prizes
✏️ Lots of spelling mistakes
🔗 Weird-looking website links
❓ Asking for passwords or money
